Image Enhancement

Why is image enhancement important?

Image enhancement is the process of improving the quality of an image, making it easier to see and understand. It can be used for a variety of purposes, including medical imaging, astronomy, photography, and web design. Some common methods of image enhancement include contrast enhancement, brightness enhancement, noise reduction, sharpening, and color correction.
